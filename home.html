Welcome to AniCalls!

an idea based on an experiment from the Animal-computer interaction researcher, Ilyena Hirskyj-Douglas.

We proudly present the first social network for your pets!
Does it make you sad to leave your pet (or pets) alone at home when you go to work? As of now, let them build their own social life through calls with other animals of their choice!
By joining us, you’ll be guided every step of the way to help your pets become more independent while keeping your peace of mind.
On top of that, we offer Bluetooth accessories equipped with sensors that allow your pets to use the calling function whenever they want kind of like a remote control
Join us on this adventure!
Here, it’s not just about cats and dogs, we also have parrots our most active Anicalls users! , as well as pigeons, hedgehogs, monkeys, and many other fun and exotic species! All of this for a maximum of FUN!

So… are you ready to take your pet on this adventure?
